# Background Prompts — Stella Overflow, Acte 1

Prompts à donner à ChatGPT (DALL-E / GPT-4 image) pour générer les 10 backgrounds nécessaires à l'Acte 1.

---

## Style de base (à rappeler dans chaque prompt)

**Common style block** — copier-coller au début de chaque prompt :

```
Anime / manhwa background art, contemporary Japan urban setting, no characters, no people, background only (for a visual novel — characters will be composited on top).
16:9 aspect ratio, 2D flat composition, centered framing, no parallax layers.
Soft pastel palette: pale pink, lavender, beige, muted gold. Subtle pixel grain texture, light painterly brush strokes.
Influences: mid-2000s shoujo anime backgrounds, Makoto Shinkai light treatment, slight goth melancholy undertone.
High detail, clean lines, volumetric light, no blur, no bokeh overuse.
```

## 1. `couloir-univ` — University corridor (day)

```
[style block]

A quiet university corridor in a modern Japanese university, seen from a stationary camera at head height. Long hallway with glossy off-white tiled floor reflecting soft daylight, pale cream walls with a wooden dado, a row of beige lockers on the right, large windows on the left letting in warm diffuse morning light. A few scattered cork notice boards with exam posters in Japanese. Visible ceiling strip lighting, switched off (daylight is enough). The hallway extends into vanishing point depth. Empty — no people. Mood: calm, slightly melancholic, ordinary.
```

## 2. `toit-univ` — University rooftop (late afternoon)

```
[style block]

The rooftop of a Japanese university building, late afternoon / early evening. Concrete flat roof with a low safety railing around the perimeter. A single rusted water tower in the background. HVAC units and a small maintenance shed on the left. Sky gradient from soft apricot at the horizon to dusty lavender above. City skyline visible in the distance (low-rise Tokyo suburb, not skyscrapers). One small puddle reflecting the sky. No people. Mood: private, liminal, the kind of place students sneak onto.
```

## 3. `cafeteria-univ` — University cafeteria (midday)

```
[style block]

A university cafeteria interior, midday. Long tables with pale wooden tops and tubular metal legs, red plastic chairs. Vending machines along the far wall (Japanese drinks brands, stylized, not logos). Tray return station on the right. Large windows letting in flat overhead daylight. Scattered abandoned trays with empty bowls and chopsticks — evidence of people having been here but no one visible. Slightly overexposed, fluorescent neutral lighting mixed with daylight. Mood: mundane, slightly unsettling in its emptiness.
```

## 4. `exterieur-soir` — University exterior, evening

```
[style block]

The plaza outside a Japanese university main entrance, early evening (golden hour / blue hour transition). A wide paved area with a few stone benches, two small cherry trees (not in bloom — late spring leaves), a bike rack with a few bicycles parked. The university building rises in the background with lit interior windows glowing warm yellow. Streetlamps just turning on, casting soft amber pools on the pavement. Sky is a deep lavender-pink gradient. No people. Mood: suspended, after classes end, the space between day and night.
```

## 5. `rue-soir` — Residential street, evening

```
[style block]

A quiet residential street in a Japanese suburb, early evening. Narrow two-lane road, no cars. Low concrete apartment buildings on both sides (3-4 stories, balconies with laundry rails), small family houses mixed in. A vending machine glowing on the corner. Power lines and transformers crisscrossing the sky. A lone streetlamp casting a warm pool of light on the asphalt. Sky is dusty purple fading to indigo. Soft pink clouds. No people. Mood: calm, intimate, the hour when daily life quiets.
```

---

## 6. `rue-incident` — Street with a distress moment

```
[style block]

Same quiet Japanese residential street at evening (consistent with rue-soir, slightly further along). Narrow road between low apartment buildings. A streetlamp overhead with a cone of warm light, but the ambient light around it feels colder, more sterile than it should be. An empty vending machine glow in the background. The air feels heavy — subtle darker purple haze thickening in one zone of the composition (bottom-left quadrant), as if the atmosphere itself is dense there. No people. Mood: wrong, something is off, the street is the same but it isn't.
```

## 7. `rue-manifestation` — Street with dark manifestation

```
[style block]

Same Japanese residential street, evening. The atmosphere has shifted darker: a dense swirling cloud of black-violet smoke / shadow energy occupies the center-left of the composition, about the size of a person, pulsing and tendril-like, floating at chest height. The streetlamp's warm light is overpowered — its cone is eaten at the edges by the darkness. The sky above is no longer lavender but deep violet with a few sickly green undertones. Shadows on the buildings stretch unnaturally long. Subtle sparks of pastel pink / lavender light drifting in the air around the dark mass (Stella's magical residue). No people. Mood: supernatural, dangerous, beautiful and wrong at once.
```

## 10. `toit-nuit` — University rooftop at night (cliffhanger)

```
[style block]

The university rooftop at deep night. Same layout as toit-univ (water tower, safety railing, HVAC units, maintenance shed) but now lit only by a distant orange sodium streetlamp far below and a thin cold moonlight from above. The city lights in the distance are a carpet of small warm dots. Sky is deep blue-black with wispy clouds. A subtle darker silhouette shape suggested on the far side of the rooftop, near the railing — ambiguous, could be a figure, could be an HVAC unit (leave it ambiguous in the composition, slightly off-center right). Mood: wrong, watched, the climax of an unsettling night.
```

## Variantes optionnelles (à générer si temps)

### `couloir-univ-soir` — Corridor, late afternoon variant
Même prompt que `couloir-univ` mais lumière dorée couchante par les fenêtres, ombres longues, couloir plus chaud et intime.

### `rue-soir-contamine` — Street evening, contaminated variant
Même prompt que `rue-soir` mais désaturation progressive, lumières des lampadaires légèrement plus blanches/froides — signal subtil de contamination (pour fin Acte 1 si Stella corrompue).

---

## Workflow conseillé

1. Générer chaque BG en 1920×1080 (ou 2048×1152) pour garder de la marge
2. Faire 3-4 variations par prompt, garder la meilleure
3. Passe finale dans Photoshop : ajuster niveaux, ajouter légère texture grain pixel uniforme sur tout le set pour cohérence
4. Exporter en `.png` dans `public/backgrounds/` sous les noms exacts ci-dessus (ex: `public/backgrounds/couloir-univ.png`)
5. Vérifier dans le jeu que chaque BG fonctionne avec les sprites Stella/Y/N/Lunae composés dessus
